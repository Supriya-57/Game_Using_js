🕹️ How to Play Rock-Paper-Scissors

  • Choose one: ✊ Rock, ✋ Paper, or ✂️ Scissors.
  • The computer will also pick randomly.
  • The winner is decided based on these rules:
Your Choice Computer’s Choice Result
✊ Rock ✂️ Scissors ✅ You Win!
✋ Paper ✊ Rock ✅ You Win!
✂️ Scissors ✋ Paper ✅ You Win!
✊ Rock ✋ Paper ❌ You Lose!
✋ Paper ✂️ Scissors ❌ You Lose!
✂️ Scissors ✊ Rock ❌ You Lose!
✊ Rock ✊ Rock 🔵 Draw!
✋ Paper ✋ Paper 🔵 Draw!
✂️ Scissors ✂️ Scissors 🔵 Draw!

Try to beat the computer! Good luck! 🎮
